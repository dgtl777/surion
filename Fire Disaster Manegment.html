Welcome to wildfirehelp.com, a global movement of millions of people coming together regardless of country, religion, race or gender to make positive change, for those who are impacted by wild fire. Wild fire does not care who it impacts in which country, as the world is getting warmer and with drier spell, we are now seeing more wild fire happening around the world more frequently.


Fires can destroy lives and livelihoods in the blink of an eye, regardless of whether they stay confined to one house or spread throughout a wide area. When the fire victims are people you know, offers of personal help can mean a lot. Monetary donations are well-received and often go farther than other types of contributions because they use the money for all kinds of needs. If you want to help fire victims you don't know, you can usually donate money through our website - which is then used for food, or supplies through third party organizations, Like the American Red Cross, Doctors without Borders, UNICEF and most importantly local charities and religious institution like Temples, Church, Mosque and Synagogue.


We need every human to help in any way you can whether that your spare time, money, or your expertise. we want to hear from you.
